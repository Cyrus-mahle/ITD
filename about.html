About Us

iTechDigital (Pty Ltd), founded in 2024 by Nyakama Mahle, is a dynamic and innovative company located in the Eastern Cape, South Africa, with offices in Gqebehra and Ntabankulu. We are dedicated to providing top-notch services in:

  • Computer Repair
  • Cellphone Repair
  • Web Development
  • Computer Science Tutoring

Our tutoring services are offered remotely across South Africa and in-person at our Mthatha and Gqebehra locations. Additionally, we sell a wide range of computer and cellphone accessories to meet all your tech needs.
